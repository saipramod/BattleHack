Due to lack of funding, many students in the Chicago area lose an important opportunity to engage in fine arts. This program encourages students from the 1st grade to the 12th. With your $1 donation you will be providing art supplies to many young artists. Most importantly your each submission is kept in our gallery archive!

Your donation will go towards opportunities for fine arts to be a part of students lives. These opportunities include after school events at open venues (YMCA) and providing students with art supplies.
